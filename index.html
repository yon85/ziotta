How Does Ziotta Works

Ziotta is open access to digital money and data-friendly services for everyone â€“ no matter your background or location. It's a community-built technology behind the cryptocurrency and thousands of applications you can use today.

Ziotta enables the buying and selling of different cryptocurrencies between users. 0.3% in fees is charged for facilitating each swap, with 0.25% going to liquidity providers and 0.05% being converted to ZTTA and distributed to users holding the ZTTA token.
